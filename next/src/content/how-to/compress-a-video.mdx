### 1. Use an Online Tool

**Best for:** Quick compression, no software install.

- Visit [Vert.sh](https://vert.sh/), [Clideo](https://clideo.com/compress-video), or [VEED.IO](https://www.veed.io/tools/compress-video), and upload your video.
- Set your preferred quality or size.
- Download the compressed video.

---

### 2. Compress on Windows or Mac

- Use free apps like **HandBrake** or **VLC**.
- Open your video, choose a lower resolution/bitrate, and export.

### Quick Tips

- Lower the resolution to 720p or 480p for email/sharing.
- Shorten clips or trim unused sections.
- Export with the H.264 codec for best size/quality balance.
